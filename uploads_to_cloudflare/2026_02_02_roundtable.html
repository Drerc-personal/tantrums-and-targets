Our prompt generator was outputting "GTC limit order at $TARGET" regardless of current price. When MSFT trades at $423 and we say "GTC at $450," that's a buy limit above market‚Äîit fills instantly. Not a waiting order. Not discipline.

The Fix (deployed today):

2. ELIGIBLE TO BUY price ‚â§ target AND not owned Can buy now Prior close
3. ELIGIBLE TO ADD price ‚â§ add_target AND owned Can add now Prior close
4. HOLD ZONE price between targets AND owned Wait for ADD N/A

The Golden Rule: NEVER place a buy limit above the prior day's close. Ever.
